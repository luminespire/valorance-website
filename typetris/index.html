Typetris

  • This is currently a proof of concept! Many more features to come...
  • Type words to build combos.
  • Press Enter to send your combo as an attack to your opponent.
  • Making a mistake will also send your current combo.
  • Don't let your grid reach the top!
  • Dev notes: The opponent right now is your WPM + base WPM + randomly sending lines.

    If you have any feature ideas, DM me on Discord: val0rance

    Attacks currently send exactly your combo (the amount of words you typed correctly consecutively). In the future, they will have multipliers.
